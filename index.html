Cornell Idea Diary Smartphone App Download Page
iPhone/iPad version 1.0.0
Install iOS version 1.0.0

After downloading the app, go to Settings > General.
Scroll down to Device Management, and then click on it.
You will see Northwestern University.
Click on Northwestern University and then click on Trust "Northwestern University".

Android version 1.0.0
Install Android version 1.0.0

After downloading the app, go to Settings > Applications > Unknown Sources.
